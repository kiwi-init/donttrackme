don't track me

Strip tracking parameters from every URL you visit. Local. Silent. Yours.

Open Settings > Apps > Safari > Extensions and turn on don't track me. Then tap the aA menu in Safari and grant the extension access to all websites.

Enable the extension in Safari > Settings > Extensions, then set its website permission to Allow on Every Website.
